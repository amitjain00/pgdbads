We can see that the data is stationary at 95% confidence since the p-value is less than $\alpha$ = 0.05.

We see that the ACF cuts-off at lag 2. The ACF line is below the 95% confidence interval line. The significant lag after which the ACF cuts-off is 1.

We see that the PACF cuts-off at lag 3. The PACF line is below the 95% confidence interval line. The significant lag after which the PACF cuts-off is 2.
